Our Story

Built for our family.
Built for yours.

We have family members who drive trucks for a living. Before trucking, they were healthy and active. A few years in — overweight, high blood pressure, nervous every time a DOT physical comes around.

Not because they don't care. Because nobody ever showed them how to stay healthy out there. Every program we found was built for big fleet carriers with HR departments and corporate budgets. Not for the independent driver.

One of us is a Registered Nurse. One of us builds digital brands. Together we built the resource we wish existed — practical, nurse-led, designed for real life on the road.

Because your health is your CDL. Your CDL is your livelihood. The road shouldn't cost you your health — or your career.
